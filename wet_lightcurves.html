XCOV 31 Lightcurves

G29-38

A wrapped plot showing the G29-38 visible data taken over the full XCOV 31 campaign, from October 13th through November 10th. Each subplot spans two days and shows the optical observation data organized by telescope/observatory that gathered the data. The LCO 1 meter data was taken by several telescopes at the follwoing Las Cumbres observatory sites: Teide, CTIO, and McDonald. All of the LCO 2 meter time was gathered by Siding Springs Observatory.
